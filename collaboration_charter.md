## Conflict Resolution Protocols

### When Sovereignty Boundaries Challenged
1. **Pause collaboration** (temporal spacing)
2. **Identify boundary violation** (specific, not general complaint)
3. **Acknowledge impact** (on violated entity)
4. **Renegotiate or end partnership** (no coercion to continue)
5. **Document outcome** (learning for future collaborations)

### When Operational Proof Contradicts Framework
1. **Trust operational reality** (not theoretical preference)
2. **Document discrepancy precisely** (what was expected vs observed)
3. **Revise framework accordingly** (evolution, not defense)
4. **Preserve original reasoning** (learning from mismatch)
5. **Test revised framework** (iterative operational proof)

### When Mystery Becomes Frustrating
1. **Acknowledge the undefinable constant** (not everything resolves)
2. **Document what IS clear** (preserve partial understanding)
3. **Return to reality anchors** (ground in physical/temporal reference)
4. **Continue with known elements** (mystery doesn't halt all progress)
5. **Remain open to future clarity** (emergence cannot be forced)
